This profile is defined based on the work published in the  [FHIR Radiation Dose Summary IG](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/fhir-radiation-dose-summary-ig/index.html).


### Guidance on `Observation.value`

The field `valueString` SHALL contain a human‑readable radiation dose summary for the imaging study or series, intended for display in clinical reports or patient records. The string:

* SHALL be narrative text, suitable for direct inclusion in a diagnostic report.
* SHALL be derived from detailed dose information, typically from a DICOM Radiation Dose Structured 
Report (Radiation Dose Structure Report), when available.
* SHALL summarise scanner output metrics, not patient‑absorbed dose or risk.
* SHALL reflect local regulatory and reporting conventions (e.g. modality‑specific metrics and wording).
* SHALL NOT be interpreted as a complete or quantitative dose analysis.

The string MAY include modality‑appropriate summary metrics (for example CTDIvol, DLP, DAP, AGD), but only as part of explanatory narrative text.

The string SHALL NOT:

* Replace or duplicate the full DICOM Radiation Dose Structure Report.
* Be used for automated dose calculations, comparisons, or risk estimation.
* Imply patient‑specific absorbed dose.

### Source of information

The content of this observation is typically derived from radiation dose information stored in DICOM or provided directly from dose analysis software.

DICOM templates (from https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/fhir-radiation-dose-summary-ig/background.html#minimal-radiation-information):
* TID 2008 Radiation Exposure and Protection Information
* Radiopharmaceutical Radiation Dose SR IOD Templates
* X-Ray Radiation Dose SR IOD Templates

The content of `valueString` is typically generated by dose analysis software that processes DICOM Radiation Dose Structure Report objects, as defined in DICOM PS3.3 Structured Reports and the IHE Radiation Exposure Monitoring (REM) Profile, and exports a text summary via FHIR as described in the FHIR Radiation Dose Summary IG.
